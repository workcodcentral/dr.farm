About the workshop

From Raw Steel
to Running Machine.

Dr. Farm Fabricators & Engineering Works is a machine design and engineering business based in Kariobangi, Nairobi — on Kamunde Road near Bishop Otunga Road, close to the roundabout. We design, fabricate and commission agro-processing machines that power farms, mills and businesses across Kenya.

Our motto is Quality Through Integrity. Every machine that leaves our workshop — posho mill, silage chopper, sugarcane crusher or feed mixer — is built to specification, tested before dispatch, and backed by our workshop warranty.
